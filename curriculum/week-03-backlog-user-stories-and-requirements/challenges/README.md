# Week 3 — Challenges

Two open-ended problems, no single right answer. Where the exercises checked recall and technique, these test judgment — turning a messy real-world input into a defensible backlog decision, the actual job of a PM or PO.

## How to work these

- Each challenge is deliberately underspecified in places. That's not an oversight — deciding what's ambiguous, stating your interpretation, and defending your call *is* the skill being tested.
- Write your reasoning down, not just your final backlog. A confident priority order with no stated rationale is a weaker submission than one that shows its work.
- There's no answer key. Compare your reasoning against the rubric in each challenge, and against a classmate's or mentor's if you can.
