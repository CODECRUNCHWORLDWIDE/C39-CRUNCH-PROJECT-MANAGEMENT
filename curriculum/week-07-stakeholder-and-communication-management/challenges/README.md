# Week 7 — Challenges

Two open-ended problems, no single right answer. Where the exercises checked recall, these test judgment — the actual job of a PM when two people disagree or the news is genuinely bad.

## How to work these

- Each challenge is deliberately underspecified in places. That's not an oversight — deciding what's ambiguous and stating your interpretation *is* the skill being tested, same as the ambiguous-questions challenges in other Crunch courses.
- Write your reasoning down, not just your final deliverable. A confident status update or a confident alignment plan with no stated assumptions is a weaker submission than one that shows its work.
- There's no answer key. Compare your reasoning against the rubric in each challenge, and against a classmate's or mentor's if you can.
